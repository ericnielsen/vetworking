Get Employed!

Connecting to other veterans is usefull, but Vetworking is ultimately about asissting veterans in finding and securing jobs.In addition to offering social networking services, Vetworking offers several types of information ranging from information about job fairs, articles about finding employment and links to other websites whose purpose is to help you find employment.

Resources
VA Careers
VA Careers is designed to help you build the career you want at the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). Whether you already work at VA, or want to join, this is your place to set and achieve career goals
Feds Hire Vets
Feds Hire Vets is a critical component of the Federal Government’s strategy for the recruitment and employment of Veterans. They provide a provide consistent and accurate information and useful training and other resources in a way that will inform both the applicant and the hiring agency.
E-Benefits
E-Benefits is the result of a collaboration between the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) and the Department of Defense (DoD). They serve Veterans, Service members, Wounded Warriors, their family members, and their authorized caregivers.
Hire our Heroes
Hire our Heroes effectively trains veterans in the skills of self-marketing, then supports their career search until they find good jobs with great companies. Signature services also include group workshops and connecting the job seeker with veteran-friendly companies and employment opportunities through the Hire Heroes USA Job Board.
USA Jobs
The Office of Personnel Management in support of the President’s Veterans Employment Initiative, created a one-stop web site resource for Federal veteran employment information.USA jobs ensure veterans, transitioning service members, and their families receive accurate and consistent information regarding veteran’s employment in the Federal Government.
Veteran Jobs Center
As a veteran, you deserve a dedicated job center that's easily accessible, costs nothing, and features employers who seek former military employees. Now you have just such a tool in this special website from Stars and Stripes, the only news media inside the Department of Defense that reports independently, free of censorship and control.

What is vetworking?
Every year, hundreds of thousands of veterans leave the military. The transition to civilian life can be difficult, especially without adequate information and resources. There is over a 17% unemployment rate among veterans aged 18-24. Vetworking gives veterans a social media platform to connect with other veterans to network and provide professional assistance.
What's in it for the employers?
Besides the professionalism, discipline, and work ethic that is typically instilled in our veterans, there are tangible business reasons why it is advantageous for companies to hire veterans. Companies often times receive tax breaks for hiring veterans. Many veterans served in military occupations that directly correlate to civilian ones. Vetworking allows companies to easily and efficiently locate employees that suit their needs.
